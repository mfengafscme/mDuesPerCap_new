12 Month Rebate Amount Add Year Help



The 12 Month Rebate Amount Add Year screen allows the user to add the current year's 12 Month Rebate Amount for Political Rebates.

The Rebate Percentage Amount (text box), Full-Time Amount (text box), Part-Time Amount (text box), Lower Part-Time Amount (text box), and Retiree Amount (text box) are required fields.

Available actions on this screen are based on the user's privileges. Key actions available on this page include:

Submit (button) The system performs validations and saves the new year.
Reset (button) The system clears any changes made to the screen returning it to its original content.
Cancel (button) The system returns to the 12 Month Rebate Amount screen without the new year saved.
